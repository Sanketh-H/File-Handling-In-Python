A professional engineer is competent by virtue of his/her fundamental education and
training to apply the scientific method and outlook to the analysis and solution of engineering problems.
He/she is able to assume personal responsibility for the development and application of engineering science
and knowledge, notably in research, design, construction, manufacturing, superintending, managing,
and in the education of the engineer. His/her work is predominantly intellectual and varied and
not of a routine mental or physical character. It requires the exercise of original thought and 
judgment and the ability to supervise the technical and administrative work of others
